I am a PhD candidate under the supervision of Associate Professor Jan Zika in the School of Mathematics and Statistics at UNSW Sydney. I completed my Honours in Applied Mathematics at UNSW Sydney in 2021. 

My research focuses on statistical modelling of marine heatwaves and compound marine extreme events, combining statistics, data science, oceanography and climate science to better understand, detect and predict environmental extremes.
